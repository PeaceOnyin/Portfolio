AVOIDING DEPRESSION

Life is about bringing out the humanity, make every moment of your life count like you are in control of it while it last. the next moment might be unpredictable, put on a glow always, and be strong.
"Every man has his secret sorrows which the world knows not, and often times we call a man cold when he is only sad. there are wounds that never show on the body that are deeper and more harmful than anything that bleeds"

let your source of happiness be within your internal locus of control, and never forgetyou only live once You alone can be the manager of your emotions, never let it go down, stay positive alw,ays..
